I used to joke that snowboarding was my first and only love, but then photography found its way to my life and I got started by simply documenting my ski trips to far away locations.

Slowly but steadily, friends of friends started asking me to tag along to film their passion in action, but it wasn't until I was approached by my first board brand that I realized I had a business.

Now, I’m running my own sports photography company and frequently get to travel to those rugged corners of the world that I love.
